Inspiring Quotes

Description

Sometimes we feel like there is no energy left to fight back the difficult moments in our lives. And there are also moments we may feel demotivated to do what needs to be done. Inspiring Quotes can help you anyway! The most important mental shift we need to make in our life is to take responsibility for our lives and have the courage to turn them into the life we want to live. With 365 carefully curated quotes to inspire you to make a shift in your life, Inspiring Quotes can boost your energy and motivation daily for a whole year with no subscription.

Download only on the App Store

Configurations

We recommend that you allow notifications for Inspiring Quotes on your device to have the best experience, it is just once a day, and you can choose what time works best for you. You can set the preferred language (English or Brazilian Portuguese) as well as the Notification Time in the Settings. Also in Settings, you can buy the premium version of Inspiring Quotes (one-time purchase), restore your purchase, and leave feedback.

Search Functionality

If you’ve seen a quote in the app before and want to share it, or if you are looking for a quote from a particular author, you can use the Search Quotes View and look for what you want. It is easy, and you can always share what you’ve found.

Share Functionality

It feels so good to be inspired that we want to inspire others. Our built-in share functionality makes it easier. You can share from Quotes List View, from Search Quotes View, or the Quote of the day. If you find a quote in Quotes List View or Search Quotes View, you can swipe to share or long-press the quote you want to share.
